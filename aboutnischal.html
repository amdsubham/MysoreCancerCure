ABOUT

Dr Nischal Raj L (Surgical Oncologist)

He is recognised for his best cancer treatment in Mysore. He have been providing breakthrough medical solutions to the patients in the field of cancer. He is widely experienced in open and advanced laparoscopic cancer surgeries. Dr Nischal Raj has been awarded Gold medal in MBBS By Rajiv Gandhi University Of Health Sciences, Karnataka in OBG. He has also been awarded Gold medal for Fellowship in minimal access surgery course in world laparoscopy hospital, Gurgaon - conducted by world association of laparoscopic surgeons. He stood 2nd rank in his MCh surgical oncology at Kidwai cancer institute, Bangalore conducted by Rajiv Gandhi University Of Health Sciences, Karnataka.


Nischal's Vision :

I wish to make comprehensive cancer care affordable to all patients in Mysore irrespective of their socioeconomic background. I am working to ensure that all cancer patients are treated in compassionate manner during this most challenging period of their lives. I will strive to ensure that the patient gets the best possible treatment and support the near and dear ones of these patients emotionally during the testing times. Finally with all support and wishes, want to make Mysore one of the top surgical cancer care center in India.
